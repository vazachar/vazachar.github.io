About me

Hey! I'm Victoria, a 23 year old ex-National Geographic Photographer. Over the last 5 years I have had the privledge to work along side National Geographic to Capture moments across the world within my favourite braches of Photograhy. My main focus of photography within my last 5 years has been wildlife, landscape, the sky and flowers.
